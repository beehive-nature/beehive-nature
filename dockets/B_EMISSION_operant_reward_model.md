# b Emission — the Operant Reward model

`b` is transferable Vaulta-native energy (mint-on-qualifying-action, burn-on-use,
spendable). This note governs *emission* — how new b is created and attributed to
a human.

## The constant
**420 b — lifetime maximum EMISSION per unique attested human.** Emission ≠
holdings (b is transferable). Cap = cumulative b ever *minted to* one verified
human. **Supply consequence:** max supply = (confirmed unique humans) × 420 — the
money is pinned to verified life.

## The emission function (founder rulings 2026-07-16)
> "respect + attestation-confidence score = added weight for increase rate of b
> unlocked over the unique attested human's life."

Four separable roles:
1. **Ceiling (420)** — hard per-unique-human lifetime max.
2. **Operant curve** — the *schedule*: front-loaded, first/early actions emit
   most, marginal emission diminishes toward 420. Rewards early engagement,
   flattens against grinding.
3. **Unlock weight = g(Respect + PoUL attestation-confidence gestalt)** — both
   accumulate over the attested life; higher weight ⇒ b unlocks *faster*.
4. **PoUL vesting gate (anti-Sybil)** — emission for a not-yet-confirmed-unique
   stream is **held (unvested), never denied**; vests as confidence confirms a
   real unique human.

## RULED — rate, not amount (velocity; the egalitarian, anti-coercion reading)
**Every confirmed unique human reaches the same 420 over life; Respect +
confidence change only *how fast*.** Your destination is guaranteed and universal;
attestation changes speed and vesting-timing, never the lifetime total. This is
the strongest anti-coercion posture — no one is starved or out-earned by
declining to attest/contribute more; the reward for attestation is *earlier*
access to your own universal allotment, not *more* of it.

## Anti-coercion guardrail (carried from the data-commons charter)
The ceiling is universal; the baseline rate (zero extra attestation) must still
deliver 420 over a normal lifetime — attestation *accelerates*, its absence never
*starves*. No dignity/voice/participation depends on unlock velocity. bLOVErAi may
propose faster unlock from confidence (its toolbox), never pressure; declining
costs nothing held.

## BNR Treasury — collateralization & release schedule (founder-stated 2026-07-16; confirm details)
The **BNR Treasury** is a smart contract that **dispenses USD and collateralizes
b** (b is backed, not free-floating). Release is **ramped, not front-loaded**, to
match value creation as the network — and the true human lifespan — is discovered
together:
- **Year 1: ≤ 10%** · **Year 2: < 20%** · **Year 3: 30%** · **Year 4: 40%** …
- then a **normal bell curve** whose shape tracks *discovered human lifespan*
  (the emission-over-life curve and the treasury-release curve converge on the
  same lifespan model).

## Qualifying actions (what triggers operant emission)
First/early actions · ResourceProofs (lending disk/bandwidth — reconciles the
earlier "mint-on-ResourceProof") · **anonymous science-data contributions** (the
data commons), each PoUL-gated. Burn-on-use does **not** reopen emission headroom
(cap is cumulative lifetime emission, not net).
