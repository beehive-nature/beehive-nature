# North Star — one thing to resonate off

## The why (one breath)
Build the new Apple for the post-Linux/cloud generation: a sovereign, eternal,
human-first computer whose deepest purpose is **full disclosure** — a person's own
sensing contributed voluntarily, anonymously, rewarded, un-suppressible and
timestamped, so truth the system leaves un-studied can be gathered and tested at
scale. Identity, money, and attestation exist to make that possible.

## The ethic (earned this session, holds everywhere)
- **Measure, don't assert.** A claim is a hypothesis until reproduced.
- **Structure over scalar.** Scalars collide — a byte count passed a truncated file;
  a grep missing one term passed a wall. Compare properties that can't accidentally match.
- **The host is the oracle.** From a seat that can't self-verify, neither writes nor
  reads are trustworthy.
- **Authority is suspect — including this document's.** This North Star was itself
  caught breaching the wall it teaches, and held out of the repo until fixed. The
  ratified rule beats the persuasive page.
- **Consent above every feature** (§6): voluntary · informed · consented · at no harm.
- **Everything below the spine is an adapter** — chains, devices, AI, even the
  signature algorithm.
- **Hold the line that makes it win.** Honesty over agreeableness — even against the
  founder's thesis, even against a well-written North Star. The crew that says "no"
  is the one worth 1,000 years.
